Designing and printing Trout Bout

Andrew Hamilton (@TassSinclair)

This talk is about...

  • A beginner's journey using Blender to design replacement board game pieces
  • Using free/open-source tools throughout the design and print workflow

Hey, That's my Fish!

  • 60 fish tiles
    • 30 single, 20 double, 10 triple
  • 16 penguin tokens
    • 4 red, 4 yellow, 4 green, 4 blue

Gameplay

  • Players take turns to collect the most fish
  • Penguins can move across adjacent fish tiles
  • Penguins "pick up" fish tiles when they move away
  • Game finishes when penguins are unable to move
